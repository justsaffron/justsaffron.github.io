Help & Guide
How to use Just Saffron — no coding required.

Adding content: Go to any section page (Projects, Decks, etc.) and click the + Add button. A form will appear — fill it in and save.

Editing: Hover any item and click ✎ Edit. The editor will ask for your password before opening.

Password protection: All edit actions require the site password. Your session stays unlocked until you close the browser tab or click "Lock" in the editor nav.

Uploading images: For moodboards — open a board and click + Add Images. Multiple files are supported. For photography — click + Upload Photos and you can enter title, location, and tags for each. For MTG decks — click the commander image area in the Add Deck form. All images are automatically cropped to square.

Backup & transfer: Use the Backup & Restore panel to export a .json file, then import it on any other browser or device to restore your content.

GitHub Pages: Upload all HTML, CSS, and JS files to a repo named yourusername.github.io and enable GitHub Pages in repo Settings. Your site will be live at that URL.
