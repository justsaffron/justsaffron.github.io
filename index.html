Onboarding Redesign
⏱ 6 weeks
In Progress

New user drop-off within the first 3 sessions was at 62%, driven by unclear value proposition during setup.

Mapped the full activation journey, ran 12 user interviews, redesigned onboarding with progressive disclosure.

PRJ-002
Search Relevance Initiative
⏱ 4 weeks
Completed

Search queries returning irrelevant top-3 results 40% of the time, causing users to abandon discovery flows.

Partnered with ML team to reweight semantic scoring. A/B tested 4 ranking models over 2-week windows.

Recency bias was masking quality signals. Balancing freshness with relevance was the core tension.
